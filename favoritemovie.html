Plot Summary

Peppermint is a 2018 American vigilante action/thriller film. The film's plot follows a mother who transforms herself into a vigilante quest for revenge against the drug cartel that killed her husband. Riley North (the main female lead) is a banker in Los Angeles, struggling financially alongside her husband Chris. They have a 10-years-old daughter name, Carly. Chris was offered the chance by one of his friends to take part in a robbery, but he did not accept the offer, but Garcia (Drug Dealer, Murderer) thinks that Chris was part of the plan, and after brutally murdering Chris's friend he sends out three of his men to kill Chris as well. One night Riley and Chris take their daughter Carly to a carnival for her birthday. And that night Chris and their daughter get killed, and Riley was badly wounded but survives. Despite her injuries, Riley positively identifies the shooters, but a corrupt judge releases the criminal claiming lack of evidences. After that Riley vanishes; and five years later the criminals were found dead in the carnival where Chris and his daughter were killed. FBI Agent Lisa Inman goes to Los Angeles and finds out that Riley robbed the bank where she worked and moved to Asia, where she became a fighter.
